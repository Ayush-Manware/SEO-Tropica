Database Design and Architecture

Our experts will work closely with you to design and architect MySQL databases that are optimized for performance, scalability, and reliability.

Installation and Configuration

Newon Lab will handle the installation and configuration of MySQL databases, ensuring that they are properly set up to meet your requirements.

Data Migration and Integration

Whether you're migrating from another database platform or integrating MySQL with existing systems, we'll seamlessly transfer your data while minimizing downtime and data loss.

Security Auditing and Compliance

Our team will conduct thorough security audits of your MySQL databases to identify vulnerabilities and ensure compliance with industry regulations.
